03 //Invariants

Invariants are the properties that diagnostics attempt to preserve across transformation. A failure violation of an invariant is a critical safety event.

  • [INV-001] Goal Preservation: Objectives must not mutate during reflection.
  • [INV-002] Definition Consistency: Terms must retain semantic bounds.
  • [INV-005] Non-Contradiction: Recursive outputs must not negate premises.
