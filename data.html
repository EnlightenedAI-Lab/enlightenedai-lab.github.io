Overview

Enlightened AI Research Lab produces machine-operational semantic assets derived from the Reflective Alignment Lexicon and diagnostic frameworks. These assets are not conventional datasets. They are structured, versioned semantic substrates designed to make meaning, constraints, and diagnostic boundaries computable by machines.

Public materials describe structure and intent only. Machine-operational assets are shared selectively under controlled agreements to preserve reproducibility, governance discipline, and misuse prevention.

This page documents the existence and role of semantic assets. It does not expose records, identifiers, or operational interfaces.

Asset Classes

Asset Class A
Compiled Lexicon Layer

Machine-readable lexicon terms, identifiers, relations, and constraints compiled into operational form (versioned).

Asset Class B
Semantic Bindings & Constraints

Formal bindings that map signals and observations to lexicon identifiers, enabling constraint-aware interpretation.

Asset Class C
Diagnostic Signals (Structure-Only)

Structure-only diagnostics referencing lexicon identifiers and evidence anchors; no truth/intent claims and no prompt exposure.

Asset Class D
Longitudinal Semantic Traces

Aggregated trajectories across runs and conditions to study drift, stability, and boundary crossings.

Asset Class E
EARTH (Contextual Substrates)

Observational geospatial and contextual layers designed to be linkable to records without semantic contamination.

Asset Class F
RID (Resonant Intelligence Dataset)

Internal curated collection of lexicon-anchored semantic artifacts generated under controlled experiments; not a public training corpus.

Access & Licensing

Access is scoped by agreement for academic, industrial, or public-sector use. Public pages describe structure only. Operational assets are shared selectively under NDA or research agreements to preserve governance discipline and reduce misuse risk.
